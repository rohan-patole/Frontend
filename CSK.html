Chennai Super Kings

About the team:

  • Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is a professional cricket franchise based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, and competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Established in 2008, CSK has become one of the most successful and widely supported teams in the league.

  • The team is known for its consistent performance, strategic leadership, and a strong core of experienced players. MS Dhoni, one of India’s most iconic cricketers, has captained the side for most of its history, earning the team a reputation for calm, tactical leadership under pressure.

  • CSK’s home ground is the historic M. A. Chidambaram Stadium, also known as Chepauk, where the team enjoys tremendous fan support.

  • Coached by Stephen Fleming, the team emphasizes stability, team chemistry, and smart cricket.

Key Achievements:

  • 5-time IPL Champions: 2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023.

  • Champions League T20 Winner: 2010, 2014.

  • Holds the record for the most IPL playoff appearances and finals reached.
