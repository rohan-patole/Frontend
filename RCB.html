Royal Challengers Bangalore

About the team:

  • Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is a franchise cricket team in the Indian Premier League (IPL), representing the city of Bangalore, Karnataka. Established in 2008, RCB is owned by United Spirits (a part of Diageo).

  • The team is known for its strong fan base, flamboyant brand, and star-studded line-ups featuring players like Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, Chris Gayle, and currently Faf du Plessis as captain.

  • Despite being one of the most popular teams in the league, RCB has never won an IPL title, though they have reached the finals three times (2009, 2011, and 2016). The team is often known for its explosive batting but has struggled with consistency, particularly in the bowling department.

  • Their team slogan is "Play Bold," reflecting their aggressive and fearless style of play.
